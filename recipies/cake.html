Birthday Cake

Description

Black forest cake is one of the richest and tastiest cakes I have ever eaten. These cakes contain rich dark choclate and white cream. There bread is super soft and these are made to be loved by everyone!

Making a black forest cake is as easy as to eat it. However we have to follow the recipy exactly as it is, because we want our cake to be delicious, not a bad effort.

Ingridients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Cocoa powder
  • Whipped cream
  • Cherries (For decorating)

Procedure

  1. Add milk, eggs,cocoa powder, baking powder and sugar to the flour
  2. Mix it well, there should be any lums in our batter.
  3. Leave the mixure for some time. Grease your cake mould with butter and cover it with butter paper
  4. Add batter to the mould. Tap the mould twice on kitchen table to level the batter. Pre-heat the oven
  5. Keep the mould in oven and bake ffor 25 minutes. After that, check whether our bread is properly baked or not with the help of knife. If we have done, take the mould out and allow it to cool
  6. Bring the cake out of mould and cut it half horizontally from the middle.
  7. Pour some cream, choclate and level it
  8. Decorate with cherries and whatever you want!
  9. Keep in refrigerater for some time and they enjoy your cake!
