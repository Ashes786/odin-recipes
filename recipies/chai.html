Spiced Chai

Description

Chai is one of the favourite drinks in Asia. Most people cannot start their day without chai. Its rich aroma and flavour refreshes the mind.It becomes more effective when spices like cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, cloves, star anise are added to it.

Today recipy is of spiced chai. Which is very easy to make and is very delicious.

Ingregients

  • Black tea leaves
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Cloves
  • Coriander

Procedure

  1. Add black tea, spices, and sugar in water and heat until it starts boiling.
  2. Add milk and heat for 2 minutes again.
  3. Full your mug and enjoy!
